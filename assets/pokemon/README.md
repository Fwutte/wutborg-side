# Pokemon #001-#010 artwork

These SVG files are original fan-art illustrations made for Monster Battle Arena.
They do not contain ripped sprites or downloaded official artwork.

The designs use the distinguishing biology and anatomy described by the Pokemon
Pokedex and Bulbapedia:

- Bulbasaur line: blue-green quadruped, darker patches, bulb, bud, and flower.
- Charmander line: lizard body, cream underside, tail flame, Charmeleon's horn,
  and Charizard's wings.
- Squirtle line: blue turtle body, shell development, Wartortle's furry ears and
  tail, and Blastoise's twin water cannons.
- Caterpie: segmented green body, yellow underside and rings, suction feet, and
  red antenna.
